Place custom art in this folder with the names below.

Required placeholder targets:
- dog_idle.png
- bee.png
- beehive.png
- water.png
- platform.png
- panel_win.png
- panel_lose.png

Recommended art specs:
- PNG with transparent background
- 256px to 1024px on the longest side
- Dog, bee, hive: simple 2D side-view or front-view cartoon art
- Water and platform: tileable textures or clean rectangular sprites
- Win/lose panels: simple 9-slice friendly panel graphics

The current prototype uses built-in Unity shapes, so replacing these files is optional for gameplay.
